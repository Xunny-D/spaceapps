Topics of Interest:
    Light the Path
        Use Earth observations to explore how human activity and regional land-based human movement patterns may have shifted in response to COVID-19.

    Where There's a Link, There's a Way
        Find innovative ways to present and analyze integrated, real-time information about the environmental factors affecting the spread of COVID-19.

    Purify the Air Supply
        Use the ISS as inspiration and develop a system to monitor and/or purify indoor air. It is entirely up to you whether the system you design is able to be used on Earth and/or in space.

    The Isolation Solution
        Develop innovative solutions to combat social isolation.
